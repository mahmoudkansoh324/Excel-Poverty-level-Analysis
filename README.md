Average income by poverty level — a clear gradient from Extreme (~185 EGP) to Non Poor (~3,082 EGP), confirming the poverty labels track reported income closely.

Highest unemployment by governorate: New Valley (58.0%), Red Sea (56.9%), Damietta (54.5%), Port Said (54.5%), Qena (54.2%).

Lowest average income by governorate: Aswan, Damietta, Ismailia, Qalyubia, and Giza rank as the five poorest by average income.

Housing tenure by poverty level: households across every poverty level are split fairly evenly between informal/squatter housing, owned housing, and rented housing — informal housing is not concentrated only among the poorest groups.

Survey volume by month: response volume fluctuates through the year, peaking in March (618 responses) and dipping in September (540 responses).
